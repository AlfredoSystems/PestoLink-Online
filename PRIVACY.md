# Privacy Policy

**App:** PestoLink  
**Developer:** AlfredoSystems  
**Effective date:** 2026-05-05

## Summary

PestoLink does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It is a robot controller that communicates directly between your device and your robot over Bluetooth.

## Data We Collect

We collect no data. PestoLink does not:

- Create or require an account
- Collect your name, email address, or any other personal information
- Track your location
- Use analytics or crash reporting services
- Send any data to our servers or any third party

## Bluetooth Permission

PestoLink requests access to Bluetooth in order to discover and connect to your robot. On Android, this also requires the Location permission, which is a system requirement for Bluetooth scanning — it is not used to determine your location and no location data is recorded or transmitted.

Bluetooth communication happens entirely between your device and your robot on your local network. No data is sent to the internet.

## Data Storage

Any settings you configure (such as gamepad preferences) are stored locally on your device only and never leave it.

## Children's Privacy

PestoLink does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this location with a revised effective date.
